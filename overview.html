Before we go

A short list to handle early

A few things sell out or need lead time. Knock these out and the rest is easy.

✓Passports
Valid with plenty of time left. Nate's appointment is set.
✓International Driving Permit
From AAA in the US — required for any driving or go-karting.
✓JR Pass
Buy before flying; covers all bullet trains between cities.
✓Suica card
Add to a phone wallet for trains and convenience stores.
✓Book the sell-outs
teamLab, Ghibli Museum, Super Nintendo World, and the ryokan all go fast.
✓Hold one flex day
Weather insurance — skip non-refundable hotels in typhoon season.
